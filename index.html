MAZE RUNNER!
Can you save this legendary programmer from the clutches of his diabolical mentees? The world depends on you!



INSTRUCTIONS


1 - the maze is guarded by his mentees! touching them will result in loss of health

2 - These ruthless mentees also fire bullets! Make sure you dodge them

3 - You can use SpaceBar to shoot these ruthless mentees!

4 - Direct the mentor through the infinitely long maze

5 - Uh-Oh!..the walls can also squash him at the left end!



Press any key to get started!
